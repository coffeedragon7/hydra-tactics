About this tool

A team advisor for Hero Wars: Alliance Hydra battles. Pick a hydra head and difficulty, build a team from your roster, and get a counter-coverage score plus reasoning grounded in the official Hydra Battle Guide.

What the score is: a relative measure (0–100) of how well your team covers the threats specific to that hydra head. Higher is better for that head — it is not a damage prediction. Actual damage depends on hero levels, gear, glyphs, artefacts, skin bonuses, pets, energy timing, and the per-attack damage cap.

What it tracks: log your actual damage after each battle and the History tab shows your trend per hydra/team. Over time this builds your ground truth for what works.

All data is local. Your roster and history are saved in your browser only. Sharing this tool with a guildmate doesn't share your data with them.
